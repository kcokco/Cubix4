Chocolate Chip Cookies are a classic dessert.
They require flour, sugar, butter, eggs, and chocolate chips.
Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
Cream the butter and sugar in another bowl.
Add eggs and vanilla extract to the wet mixture.
Combine wet and dry ingredients carefully.
Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
Let them cool before serving.
